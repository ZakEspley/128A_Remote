Course Info

For this course, you will need a few widely available materials. Please ensure that you have these items, or an acceptable alternative by the first week of labs. We provide a potential amazon purchase option for each item, but you may be able to find some items cheaper at your local hardware store, as indicated.

Materials List

A Circuit Kit

All students need a circuit kit that contains, at minimum:

  • A solderless breadboard
  • Wires
  • 5V/3.3V power block
  • Resistors
  • Capacitors
  • Various powered components such as LEDs.
This course was built to work with the Rexqualis Fun Kit, available on Amazon for approximately $15. We reccomend purchasing this kit in particular, to ensure you have all the components needed for the course.

A Multimeter

In order to make electrical measurements, you will need to have a digital multimeter. Any multimeter will work. We suggest the AstroAI DM130B which is sold on Amazon for approximately $16, and has an auto-ranging feature.


A cheaper version is the AstroAI AM33D which costs about $12. This version does not have auto-ranging, so you will have to turn the dial to the appropraite measurement scale.

Additional Supplies

Stong Magnets

You will need some strong magnets, such as these neodynium magnets . Note that you can usually find these for $2-$3 at any hardware store.
